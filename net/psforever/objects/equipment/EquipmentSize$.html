    Perform custom size comparison.

    In almost all cases, the only time two sizes are equal is if they are the same size. If either size is Blocked, however, they will never be equal. If either size is Inventory or Any, however, they will always be equal. Size comparison is important for putting Equipment in size-fitted slots, but not for much else.
